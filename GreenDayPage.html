Green Day


My Thoughts

I was personally introduced to Green Day through their 2004 album American Idiot through a friend that was listening to it. I never personally liked this album as much, especially after I went on to find their 1991 album Dookie. Dookie sounded better to me as it was more like the music I was used to at the time-- (punk) rock akin to Nirvana and Offspring. American Idiot, though still not necessarily "bad", was still more along the lines of pop punk and was arguably more "emo" than my own personal taste. After Dookie, Nimrod, and Insomniac I feel as if the albums gradually get worse and farther from the original style the band started with. Even so, they continue to be one of my favorite bands that I listen to among the most often.
